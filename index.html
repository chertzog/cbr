Crude By Rail

Intro

What is it?

Crude by rail (CBR) is the term used for shipping crude oil...

by rail

Why is it important?

CBR fills the gaps left in the pipeline network caused by:

  • Providing service to areas without pipeline terminals
  • Transporting excess supply during pipeline congestion
  • Faster service is needed

Transportation Modes for Crude Oil

History

Crude by rail was almost non-existent
